Key Takeaways for Copy

Positioning: "The copilot for your design system" — not an HTML-to-Figma tool, not a screenshot converter, not another AI frame generator. A copilot that uses your real DS and learns over time.

Hero differentiator: Learning. No competitor does this. Corrections become rules. Patterns auto-verify. Knowledge compounds. This is the headline.

Secondary differentiator: DS compliance on delivery. No cleanup pass. Real components, real tokens, real auto-layout. Output is production-ready.

Tertiary differentiator: DS audit. Every build reveals what's missing. Mimic doesn't just build — it reviews your design system.

Empathy hook: "You spent months building a design system. Then someone needs a screen in Figma and builds it from scratch." Every DS lead has felt this.

Category creation: "Design system copilot" is an unoccupied category. No competitor claims it. Mimic should own this term.
